Different Types of Indian Sweets

Gulab Jamun

Soft milk-solid balls soaked in sugar syrup.

Jalebi

Crispy spiral-shaped sweet soaked in sugar syrup.

Rasgulla

Spongy balls made from chhena soaked in syrup.

Ladoo

Round sweet made from flour, ghee, and sugar.

Kaju Katli

Diamond-shaped sweet made from cashew nuts.

Rasmalai

Soft paneer discs soaked in sweet, flavored milk.
